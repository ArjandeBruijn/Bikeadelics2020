How it works

We meet up at Jessup Farm Barrel House evening or weekend and we teach you how to ride a penny farthing.

Once you're comfortable we take a scenic round tour The trip is about 3 miles of scenic trail along the spring creek and the poudre. It is all hard pavement and you should rarely have to get off the bike. It is fun for new penny farthing riders. on the poudre trail.

We have mini-penny farthings for 8+ kids and a fun car-bike and bakfiets for kids 4-8. Younger kids can use a bicycle trailer that we can mount behind a penny farthing.
